\end{itemize}

Each Party agrees to comply with all applicable tax reporting, filing, and recordkeeping requirements arising from this Agreement, including but not limited to:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Issuance of \textbf{\textit{Donor}} acknowledgment letters;

  \item Retention of records as required by law.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{No Guarantee of Tax Benefit}
The \textbf{\textit{Donor}} understands and agrees that:
\begin{itemize}

\end{itemize}
