\section{Publicity}
\label{sec:publicity}

\subsection{Public Acknowledgment of Donation}
The \textbf{\textit{Recipient}} may publicly acknowledge the \textit{Donation}, subject to the terms of this Agreement. Such acknowledgment may include:
\begin{itemize}
  \item The \textbf{\textit{Donor}}’s name, logo, or likeness;
  \item The amount or description of the \textit{Donation};
  \item The purpose or use of the \textit{Donation}; and
  \item Any associated events, programs, or publications.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Consent Requirement}
Unless otherwise agreed in writing, the \textbf{\textit{Recipient}} shall not use the \textbf{\textit{Donor}}’s name, logo, or likeness in any public announcement, marketing materials, social media posts, press releases, or promotional activities without prior written consent from the \textbf{\textit{Donor}}.

\subsection{Scope of Publicity}
Any publicity regarding the \textit{Donation} shall comply with the following:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Accurately represent the \textit{Donation} and its purpose;
  \item Respect any restrictions on the use of the \textit{Donation} (e.g., restricted funds or designated purposes);
  \item Avoid misleading statements, exaggerations, or claims regarding the \textbf{\textit{Donor}} or the Donation.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Event and Media}
If the \textit{Donation} is recognized at an event or media appearance:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Both Parties shall mutually agree on the \textbf{\textit{Donor}}’s role or mention;
  \item The \textbf{\textit{Donor}}’s participation shall be voluntary;
  \item The \textbf{\textit{Recipient}} shall obtain prior written approval for any recordings, photographs, or media quotes using the \textbf{\textit{Donor}}’s name or likeness.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Privacy and Confidentiality}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Nothing in this Agreement shall obligate the \textbf{\textit{Recipient}} to disclose the \textit{Donation} publicly
  \item If the \textbf{\textit{Donor}} desires confidentiality, the \textbf{\textit{Recipient}} shall respect the request and refrain from any public acknowledgment or disclosure.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{No Endorsement}
Publicity of the \textit{Donation} shall not imply any endorsement or sponsorship of the Donor by the \textbf{\textit{Recipient}}, unless expressly agreed in writing.
