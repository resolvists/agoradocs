\section{Obligations of the Receiving Party}
\label{sec:obligations}

The \textit{Receiving Party} agrees to:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Use the \textit{Confidential Information} solely for the \textit{Purpose}
  \item Not disclose the \textit{Confidential Information} to any third party without prior written consent
  \item Protect the \textit{Confidential Information} with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care
  \item Limit access to \textit{Confidential Information} to those who need to know for the \textit{Purpose} and are bound by confidentiality obligations
\end{itemize}
