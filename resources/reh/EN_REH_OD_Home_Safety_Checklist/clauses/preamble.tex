\section*{\centering HOME SAFETY CHECKLIST}
\vspace*{0.5cm}

A structured home safety checklist helps reduce the risk of accidents, property damage, and emergencies. Regular inspections and preventative maintenance ensure that potential hazards—such as gas leaks, fire risks, electrical faults, and water damage—are identified and addressed promptly.
