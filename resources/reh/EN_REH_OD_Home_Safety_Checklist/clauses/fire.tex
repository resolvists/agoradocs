\section{Fire Safety}
\label{sec:fire}

\begin{tabular}{p{9cm} p{8cm}}
$\square$ Install smoke alarms on every level of the home & $\square$ Test smoke alarms monthly \\ 
$\square$ Replace batteries at least once per year & $\square$ Clean dryer lint filter after every use \\ 
$\square$ Keep fire extinguishers in kitchen, garage, and near heating systems & $\square$ Keep flammable materials away from heat sources \\ 
$\square$ Avoid overloading electrical outlets & $\square$ Inspect chimneys and fireplaces annually \\ 
$\square$ Develop and practice a home fire escape plan & $\square$ Check fire extinguisher pressure gauge regularly \\ 
\end{tabular}
